Freedom

by Jesus A Acosta

Freedom is just another word for nothing left to lose.
--Janis Joplin

The chains that keeps us from true freedom are bad experiences of the past, good experiences of the past, fears and anxieties about the future, ambitions for the future, clinging to present things, and delusions about our own importance.
--Anthony Demello

Freedom is being able to do what one wishes and desires without any restrains or not under the control of another.
