The Wisteria Tunnel

Japan has many beautiful and famous flowers. Most commonly, the Cherry Blossom, also know as the Sakura flower, is widely known for blossoming once a year. This pink flower during the Spring, is one of Japan's tourist attractions. Also, the Kintai Bridge host an annual festival for when the Cherry Blossom blooms. It is a very gorgous site and I recommend everyone to go see it.
